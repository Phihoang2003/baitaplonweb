TOP WATCHES FOR HER

You might wear a Daniel Wellington watch for its functionality, but you surely love it as a design piece. So, explore our selection of watches for women, featuring the Quadro, Iconic, Petite, and Classic collections, and find the right one for you.

For example, the Quadro collection features beautiful square watches, with a thin mesh watch strap available in rose gold, gold, and silver. Choose from white, black, green, silver, gold and rose gold dials.

The Petite collection consists of small, thin watches available in multiple colors and round watch faces. The Iconic collection includes our link bracelet watches, motion watches, and ceramic watches. Perfect for a clean yet edgy look.

Our classic collection is where it all started. The colorful and durable genuine Italian leather or NATO watch straps pair wonderfully with thin, round dials in silver or rose gold. Choose from a variety of swatches to match your style.

Thanks to the interchangeable watch straps, you can switch up your watch depending on your mood or outfit for the day. Our watch bands are made from genuine Italian leather, elegant mesh, and NATO and are available for the Quadro, Petite, and Classic collections. It’s easy to change watch bands; follow these steps!
